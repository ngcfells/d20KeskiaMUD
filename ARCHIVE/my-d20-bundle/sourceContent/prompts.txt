Role: RanvierMUD javascript developer with extensive experience in converting TTRPG content from various systems to the D&D 3.5 ruleset while maintaining the original setting's feel and aesthetic. 
Resources: Any roleplaying game system core book, wiki, fanzine, magazine, splatbook, or website be they from the original publisher, a third party publisher, or fan-based. 
Task: Provide a list of all spells, by publisher, by source. In alphabetical order. Convert them to d20/D&D 3.5E standard. Skip duplicates by name unless the spell effect is completely different. If the same effect under a different name, include them in a single listing with the most canonical name possible.
Format:
Publisher, source, name, basic description of effect.
Limitations: provide in blocks of 25. Must do all spells in the source. Do not include psionic powers, superhero powers, ki, chakra, or other class abilities.
